FAQs

Answers to the most common questions about CAPT.

Fit & Positioning
How do I know if I'm the right fit?

You are the right fit if you have a proven business, a solid offer, and are ready to scale aggressively.

We do not work with beginners or people just looking for a cool video. We partner with ambitious founders who want to dominate their niche and are ready to invest in a real, ROI-driven marketing machine.

Why is building a personal brand so important?

Because people do not buy from logos anymore; they buy from people they trust.

A strong personal brand makes you the unquestioned authority in your space. When your audience trusts your face and your voice, your conversion rates skyrocket, and you completely stop having to compete on price.

Why should my business be on social media?

Because that is exactly where your customers are spending their attention and their money.

If you are not dominating their feed with high-value content, your competitors are. Social media is not just about posting updates - it is a 24/7 sales engine that works for you even while you sleep.

Results & ROI
What kind of results can I expect?

Expect a massive shift in how your market perceives you. We do not optimize for vanity metrics; we optimize for cash flow.

You can expect higher engagement, more booked calls, a shorter sales cycle, and an audience that is actually primed and ready to buy your stuff.

How long until I see results?

While a great hook can make a video pop off in week one, real business growth compounds.

Usually, by month 2 or 3 of consistent, high-level content, the algorithm locks onto your ideal target audience and the inbound leads start rolling in passively.

How long does it take to get an ROI?

Content is a compounding asset. Most of our clients start seeing a direct ROI within the first 60 to 90 days.

The best part? The videos we engineer and post for you today will act like digital salespeople, continuing to bring you leads months down the line.

How many followers or views do I get?

We do not chase empty views; we chase buyers.

A viral video with 1,000,000 views from 13-year-olds will not make your business a single dollar. We strategically engineer your content to get in front of your specific target audience, so you get views that actually turn into revenue.

Process & Time Commitment
What do I really get?

You get an entire marketing department in a box.

You get proven video scripts based on high-converting sales funnels, elite-tier video editing, eye-catching graphics, and a dedicated team engineering every piece of content to turn viewers into paying clients.

What social media platforms do you work with?

We build total omnipresence.

We optimize and distribute your content across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Facebook, and LinkedIn. Wherever your ideal paying clients are spending their time, your brand will be right in front of them.

How much time will you need from me? (And how much do I spend on this?)

Just 1 to 2 hours a month. That is it.

Our entire system is built to buy your time back. We prepare the concepts, write the scripts, and handle the editing and distribution. You just sit down, hit record, and go back to running your business.

What if I am not happy with the videos?

We fix it immediately. But realistically, we put so much upfront effort into the strategy and the hooks that revisions are rare.

We do not just edit - we engineer the video to convert. If something is slightly off-brand, my team polishes it until it is perfect.

Investment & Alternatives
How much does it cost?

It depends entirely on your goals and how aggressively you want to scale. We build custom retainers based on the volume and strategy you need.

Look at it this way: our strategies are built so that just one or two closed deals from our content often pays for your entire monthly investment.

How long is the commitment?

We do not trap you in massive, unbreakable yearly contracts.

We operate on a month-to-month or 90-day basis because we believe in proving our value and earning your business every single month through hard results.

Should I do this in-house?

If you want to spend $60,000+ a year on a single videographer who does not understand marketing psychology or sales funnels, sure.

Partnering with us gives you access to a full team of strategists, copywriters, and elite editors for a fraction of the cost of one decent in-house employee.

Could I just hire a freelancer?

You could, but you will be managing them constantly. Freelancers just execute orders; they do not bring strategy to the table.

If you want to micromanage a cheap video editor, hire a freelancer. If you want a strategic partner who proactively scales your brand, you hire my agency.
